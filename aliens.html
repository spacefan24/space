ALIENS


Extraterrestrial life, also called alien life, is life that occurs outside of Earth. These may range from simple bacteria to intelligent beings with civilizations far more advanced than humanity. The science of extraterrestrial life is known as exobiology.


Since the mid-20th century, there has been an ongoing search for signs of extraterrestrial life. This encompasses a search for current and historic extraterrestrial life. Search methods range from analysis of telescope to radios used to detect and send communication signals.


The concept of extraterrestrial life has had a major impact in works of science fiction. Over the years, science fiction communicated scientific ideas, imagined a wide range of possibilities, and influenced public interest in and perspectives of extraterrestrial life.


In 1977, Voyager 1 and 2 were launched. Their primary mission was to visit Jupiter and Saturn. Voyager 2 continued onto Uranus and Neptune; Voyager 1 has now made its way into interstellar space to search for aliens.
